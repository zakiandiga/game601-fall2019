Game Modification: Mindful War
A modification to the traditional War card game. Unlike original War where both players cannot choose which card to use, Mindful War allows players to decide which card to reveal from the three cards on their hand. In addition, there are cards that have special abilities which can modify the board or players hand. With these modifications, players can challenge their strategic thinking ability, which hopefully adds the fun!

(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: Draft & Deckbuilding
Draft & deck building is a multiplayer card game which combines drafting and deck building as its core mechanics. The objective of the game is damage other players as much as possible. Each player starts with their own player deck, and a number of Health Point cards which is seperated from their deck. Players who lost all of the Health Point cards are out of the game. Each turns, the game consist of 4 phases which are: 1) Draft, 2) Preparation, 3) Battle, and 4) Clean-up.

(Final) Digital Game: Brain Splitter
This is a simple bullet hell game with a twist, where the player needs to control two different sprites with one command, simultaneously. Train your multi tasking ability! What Players need to do is to avoid the black obstacles that keep comming along the way, by moving left and right, relative to the sprite's direction.
